Kathryn Ellen Walton

Bio: Creative non-fiction / fiction writer

Artist Statement:

This was the first time makiong digital work. I enjoyed using colours as aesthetics and would like to further investigate this mode of work. Digital writing offers new ideas and outcomes to create works of both text and images. I made a simple twine story, based on symbolism and learning the power of self.

Something about myself:

Having completed an Advance Dipolma in Screenwriting and now 3rd year BA in Creative Writing, I am now focusing on my debut novel titled: Ain't your Business, with the hopes of turning it into a TV series. Ain't your Business is based on a diverse collective of people, in a yet to be decriminalized work force, set in Melbourne.
