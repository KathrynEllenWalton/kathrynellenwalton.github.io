Kathryn Ellen Walton

Bio:

Creative Non-Ficton // Ficton Writer Having completed an Advanced Diploma in Screenwriting and now 3rd year BA in Creative Writing, I am now focusing on my debut novel titled: 'Ain't your Business' - with the hopes of turning it into a TV series. 'Ain't your Business' is based on a diverse collective of people, in a yet to be decriminalized work force, set in Melbourne, where I am currently based.

Artist Statement:

Digital Writing is a new concept to me, which I thoroughly enjoyed, yet still trying to understand coding. I enjoyed using colours as aesthetics and would like to further investigate this mode of work. What I like about digital writing is that it offers new ideas and outcomes to create works of both text and images. I made a simple twine story, based on symbolism and learning the power of self. I have always been interested in psychology as a subtextual theme in my work and try to layer both the protagonist and the antagonist within it. Therefore, in my writing, I aim to capture the emotional intelligence level(s) and spiritual abilities of characters, as it is what makes my stories move forward.
